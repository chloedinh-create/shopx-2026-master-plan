11Mention Scoring Framework

Mỗi mention/post được score theo 3 metrics × 1-5 scale: Audience Fit × Influence × Decision Impact. Total max 15. Used để prioritize who to ask + what to amplify.

📊 Scoring criteria
Audience Fit (1-5) — How closely does their audience match ShopX ICP (ecom managers/founders/agencies/sellers)?
Influence (1-5) — How much do they actually move their audience to action (vs vanity reach)?
Decision Impact (1-5) — How directly does their post affect register decision?
Total Score = Fit + Influence + Decision Impact (max 15). Threshold: ≥12 = high priority; 9-11 = medium; <9 = nice-to-have.

14Channel Concentration Filters — anti-fragmentation guards

1M impressions hiện tại distribute qua ~127 touchpoints (25 apps + 70 influencers + 22 PR + 10 markets channels + 5 paid). Risk: ops drag từ long-tail + diluted unique reach. 5 filter dưới đây cắt 40-60% effort vào long-tail mà vẫn giữ defensibility của portfolio approach.

Min-bar imp/touchpoint (proposed)

Filter 1 · Min-bar 3K impressions per touchpoint

Rule: Bất kỳ partner / influencer / app commit < 3K impressions thì drop ở W6 checkpoint. Apply uniformly across all channel groups.
Why: Touchpoint < 3K = outreach ROI < 1.5K imp/giờ. So với Tier 1 PR (4K imp/giờ) hoặc top app warm intro (2-3K imp/giờ) — long-tail không justify ops cost.
Expected drop: ~10 of 25 apps (bottom tier), ~30 of 70 influencers (chủ yếu Tier 3 + Internal LinkedIn squad), ~3 of 22 PR placements.
Net impact: Nominal impressions giảm 100-120K (15%), nhưng team capacity tăng 40-50%. Re-allocate capacity vào top performers → likely +50-80K từ deeper engagement.
Owner: Chloe (decide kill list at W6 checkpoint)

Filter 2 · Bundle outreach by audience cluster

Rule: 25 apps → 5 batched conversations theo category (Reviews / Email · Loyalty · CRO · Analytics). 1 outreach lead per cluster. Same approach cho 70 influencers (cluster theo audience overlap).
Why: 25 individual conversations × ~4hrs each = 100hrs. 5 batched conversations × ~6hrs each = 30hrs. Cắt 70% ops time.
Risk caveat: Loox + Judge.me cùng cluster Reviews — có thể tế nhị (competitors). Skip cluster batch khi 2 partners trong cluster là direct competitors; outreach separately.
Owner: Christine (apps clustering) · Kallie (influencer clustering)

Filter 3 · Top-decile commitment lock (70% rule)

Rule: Lock 10 sources cam kết bằng văn bản trước W6 (start of P2). These 10 sources = 70% target impressions:
  • 5 Tier 1 influencers (signed VIP exchange agreement)
  • 3 P1 apps (Judge.me + Klaviyo + PageFly — confirmed co-marketing post)
  • 2 Tier 1 PR placements (1 tech press + 1 VN media — booked interview)
Why: Concentration trên signed commitments > volume trên cold outreach. If 10 sources lock by W6 → 700K nominal impressions secured (70% of 1M) trước khi peak load.
Without filter: Team chases 127 touchpoints với 30-40% drop-off mid-campaign → scramble for replacement at W10.
Owner: Chloe (Tier 1 + PR) · Christine (P1 apps)

Filter 4 · W6 triage checkpoint (07/07)

Rule: Tại W6 (end of Influencer Map + Audience channel mapping), do 1 day triage review:
  • Kill mọi channel chưa confirmed slot (apply Filter 1 min-bar)
  • Double-down vào top 50% performers (signed commitments)
  • Re-forecast impressions/registers từ current pipeline (NOT từ sheet target)
  • If forecast < 70% of target → trigger paid budget reallocation (Filter 5)
Why: W6 = last chance để course-correct trước outreach push P2-P3 (W5-W14). Tránh sunk-cost trap với long-tail không scale.
Owner: Chloe (lead triage) · Kallie + Hellen + Christine (input forecast)

Filter 5 · UTM mandatory + unique-reach tracking

Rule: Trước W3 (29/06) — không kênh nào launch chưa có UTM. Apply tới TẤT CẢ paid + organic + earned sources.
Why: Without UTM, channel KPI là vanity number. 127 touchpoints × 0 attribution = decisions made on noise (xem Risk Register Risk #6).
Track unique-reach NOT sum-impressions: 1M impressions có thể chỉ là 600-700K unique do audience overlap (Shopify ecosystem = same buyers seeing Judge.me + Klaviyo + Triple Whale + PageFly posts). Audit overlap monthly via Kallie's HypeAuditor/Modash data.
Owner: Hellen (paid UTM) · Chloe (organic/earned UTM mandate) · Dev (pixel + tracking setup)
📊 Expected impact của 5 filters
Without filters: 127 touchpoints · 6 team members · ~40% drop-off mid-campaign · attribution noise · likely 60-70% of 1M target hit.
With filters: ~60-70 active touchpoints sau W6 cut · same team · 10 locked sources = 70% target guaranteed · tracked attribution · likely 85-100% of 1M target hit với higher unique reach.
Trade-off: Nominal "impressions count" giảm trên paper nhưng effective + tracked + qualified reach tăng.
🚦 Counter-arguments — vì sao vẫn giữ portfolio breadth
Không bỏ portfolio approach (chuyển sang top-5-only):
1. Defensibility: 1 mega-influencer fail = mất 200K. 70 influencers fail 10 vẫn còn 60K.
2. Compound FOMO: Pillar 4 "ecosystem is showing up" cần ≥15 visible partner mentions. Top-5-only không tạo được effect này.
3. ShopX 2026 = unbranded event: Không có brand equity để ride. Cần distribution-by-bundling cho first-time category leader positioning.
4. APAC community-first: WeChat groups + niche FB groups có high engagement không reflect được trong follower count.
5 filters trên = giữ portfolio nhưng cut ops drag, không phải pivot sang concentration strategy.
